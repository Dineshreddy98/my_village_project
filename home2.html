    Overview of Peddaamudyala Padu

  • Pedda Amudyalapadu is a Village in Manopadu Mandal in Jogulamba Gadwal District of Telangana State, India.
  • It belongs to Telangana region . It is located 32 KM towards South from District head quarters Jogulamba Gadwal. Manopad ( 3.5 KM ) , Vallur ( 4.5 KM ), Amaravai ( 3 KM ) , Koildinne ( 5 KM ) , Gokulapad ( 6 KM ) are the nearby Villages to Narayanapoor.
  • Pedda Amudyalapadu is surrounded by Alampur Mandal towards East , Kurnool towards South . Kurnool , Gadwal , Wanaparthy are the near by Cities to Pedda Amudyalapadu.
  • This Place is in the border of the Mahbubnagar District and Kurnool District. Kurnool District Kurnool is South towards this place .
    • It is near to the Andhra Pradesh State Border.
